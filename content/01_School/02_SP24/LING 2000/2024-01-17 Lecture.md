# Topics:
- [[Vowels]] vs [[Consonants]]
	- vowels do not have a place or manner of articulation
	- almost always voiced
	- four [[Vowels|vowel]] features
		- tongue height
		- tongue advancement
		- lip rounding
		- tenseness
	- syllabic consonants
		- consonants with the quality of a vowel
			- prism
		- **syllable (on exam)**
			- onset and rhyme
