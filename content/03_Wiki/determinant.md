The determinant is a special number that can be calculated from a **square** matrix

# 2x2 Matrix
$$A=\left[ \begin{matrix}
a & b \\
c & d
\end{matrix} \right] $$
**Determinant**: $\lvert A \rvert=ad-bc$

# 3x3 Matrix
$$A=\left[ \begin{matrix}
a & b & c \\
d & e & f \\
g & h &i
\end{matrix} \right] $$
**Determinant:** $\lvert A \rvert = a(ei-fh)-b(di-fg)+c(dh-eg)$
