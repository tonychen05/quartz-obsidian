
> [!INFO] Theorem
> If $f(x)\leq g(x)\leq h(x)$ when $x$ is near $a$ (except possibly at $a$) and $$
\lim_{ x \to a } f(x)=\lim_{ x \to a } h(x)=L,
$$ then $\lim_{ x \to a }g(x)=L$
